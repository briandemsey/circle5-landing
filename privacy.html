Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Hallucinations.cloud LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates Circle5, a family safety application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Location Data:

  • GPS coordinates from your device
  • Location accuracy and altitude
  • Speed and heading (when moving)
  • Timestamps of location updates

Device Information:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Battery level
  • Push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Location sharing with circle members GPS coordinates, timestamps
Geofence alerts Location data, geofence boundaries
Account management Email, name, profile info
Push notifications Device tokens, alert preferences
Service improvement Aggregated, anonymized usage data

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With Circle Members: Your location is shared with members of circles you join, with your consent.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third parties (hosting, analytics) who are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect safety.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase)
  • Row-level security policies for database access
  • Regular security audits

5. Data Retention

Location history is retained for 30 days by default. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of location sharing at any time
  • Export your data

7. Children's Privacy

Circle5 is designed for family use. Children under 13 may only use Circle5 under parental supervision and with parental consent. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Location Tracking Consent

Location tracking requires explicit opt-in through device permissions. You can disable location sharing at any time through your device settings or within the Circle5 app.

9. Third-Party Services

Circle5 uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Expo/React Native: Application framework
  • Apple Maps: Map display (when tracking AirTags)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through the app or email.
